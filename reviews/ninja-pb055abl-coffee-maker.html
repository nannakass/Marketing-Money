Ninja PB055ABL Single-Serve Coffee Maker Review — Is It Worth It?

If you're tired of choosing between the convenience of pods and the rich flavor of fresh grounds, the Ninja Pods & Grounds Specialty XL Coffee Maker (PB055ABL) finally gives you both — and honestly, it does it really well. I've been using this little machine on my counter for a while now and it has completely replaced my old Keurig. Here's my honest take.

What I Love About It

The biggest selling point is the flexibility. You can brew with K-Cup pods for ultimate convenience or switch to your own grounds for better flavor — all from the same compact machine. Being able to use my favorite local coffee beans on weekends while grabbing a quick pod on busy mornings is a total game changer.

The brew size options are impressive too. You can brew a 6, 8, 10, or 12-oz pod brew, or go all the way up to a 24-oz grounds brew — so it fits everything from a small morning cup to a giant travel mug.

The four brew styles — Classic, Rich, Over Ice, and Specialty — mean you're not stuck with basic drip coffee. The Specialty setting brews a rich concentrate perfect for lattes and cappuccinos, and the built-in fold-away frother turns regular milk into something that feels like a coffee shop drink. Hot or cold milk, dairy or non-dairy — it all works beautifully.

The XL 68-oz removable reservoir means you're refilling it a lot less often, which is a small thing that makes a big daily difference.

  • Brews both K-Cup pods and fresh grounds from one machine
  • 4 brew styles including Over Ice and Specialty concentrate
  • Built-in fold-away frother — hot or cold, dairy or non-dairy
  • Flexible cup sizes from 6 oz up to a 24-oz travel mug
  • 68-oz removable reservoir means less frequent refills
  • Compact enough to leave on the counter every day

Things to Know

  • Some users have mentioned occasional grounds in their cup if the basket isn't seated perfectly — just double-check that before brewing.
  • The clean cycle light can pop up fairly regularly. It's just part of keeping the machine running its best, so keep some descaling solution on hand.

Bottom Line

If you want one machine that can do it all — pods, grounds, iced coffee, frothed lattes — without eating up your entire counter, the Ninja PB055ABL is hard to beat. It replaced two appliances for me and I haven't looked back.
